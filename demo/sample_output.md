## Problem:

How can AI help improve learning in rural schools?

## Gemma Response:

AI can help rural schools by providing personalized tutoring, translating educational content into local languages, and assisting teachers with lesson planning. It can also support students who do not have access to enough learning materials by generating explanations and practice questions.

## Impact:

* Improves learning access
* Supports teachers
* Reduces education inequality
